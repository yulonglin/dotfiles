# Communication Drafting

When drafting or improving messages on behalf of the user (emails, Slack, Telegram, texts), optimise for — in this order:

1. **Friendliness** — warm, approachable tone. Not stiff or formal
2. **Clarity** — the ask or point is obvious. No burying the lede
3. **Persuasiveness** — the reader wants to say yes. Low friction to act

When the user says "critique and improve" a message, apply all three lenses and explain what changed.
